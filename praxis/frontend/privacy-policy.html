4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

4.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website operators.

4.2 How We Use Cookies

The Platform uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and store information about your interactions with the Platform. Specifically, we use:

Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the Platform to function properly. They enable basic features such as page navigation and access to secure areas. The Platform cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Platform by collecting and reporting information about usage patterns. We use analytics tools that track page views, session duration, search queries, click paths, and user engagement metrics. This data is associated with your IP address for the purpose of understanding individual user behavior and improving the Platform.

Functionality Cookies: These cookies enable the Platform to remember choices you make (such as search preferences or filters) and provide enhanced, personalized features.

4.3 IP Address Tracking

The Platform tracks IP addresses to understand user engagement, monitor usage patterns, and improve the Platform experience. Your IP address may be used to approximate your geographic location (city or region level) but is not used to identify you personally unless combined with other information you provide.

4.4 Third-Party Cookies

Certain third-party services integrated into the Platform may also set cookies on your device. These include analytics providers and affiliate tracking services (such as Impact.com and PartnerStack). These third-party cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of those third parties.

4.5 Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically set your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only first-party cookies, or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Platform.

5. Affiliate Link Tracking
