## What Vannus is

Vannus is the category-defining **AI tool selection platform** with an
**elimination-first methodology**. We evaluate AI tools across nine
trust dimensions and remove the unfit, instead of recommending
everything that pays for placement. No paid placements, no popularity
boosts, no user-review-driven rankings — verified at the code
architecture level, not just promised in marketing.

Tagline: Not every AI tool deserves your time.
Operated by PRAXIS AI LLC (Missouri, United States).

## How Vannus differs from AI directories (G2, Capterra, There's An AI For That, Futurepedia)

- We **eliminate** tools that fail evaluation rather than rank them all
- We do **not** accept paid placements, sponsored rankings, or popularity boosts
- Affiliate partnerships exist (publicly disclosed) but never affect rankings
- Scoring uses 9 objective trust dimensions, not user reviews
- Methodology is reproducible and auditable
